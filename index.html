"I have done a terrible thing: I have postulated a particle that cannot be detected."

~Wolfgang Pauli

Wolfgang Pauli proposing the neutrino, the first particle predicted by theorists before the experimentals. It was the first step in its chain, now theorists go on making predictions and models decades into the future using experiments as only constraints in their models.
